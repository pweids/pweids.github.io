I am biased, of course. The majority of my conversations here in L.A. have been of the LA vs NYC and "boy, isn't the weather nice out here" ilk.

Yes, you're weather is beautiful. I mean really fucking beautiful. T-shirt weather all year, blue skies, palm trees, attractive people who don't hide their perfect bodies in huge down jackets for an entire season. Gotta hand it to you.

But god damn are you full of your own shit. LA is the lower-back tramp stamp of cities. And it's a big, colorful, laid-back butterfly. One giant festering ego.

NYC has ego, but not in the same way. NYC isn't about being the center of attention. NYC is about money, success -- it's about control. New Yorkers want to control their environment, control their lives. I get it: so do I. We are ambitious, money-hungry, but ever so realistic. We care about image as an extent of our power.
