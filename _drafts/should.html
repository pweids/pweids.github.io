I should post on this blog more often.

I should get up earlier.
I should go to bed earlier.
I should just sleep more.

I should eat better.
I should drink less.
I should workout more.

I should date a respectable woman.
But should postpone marriage until my late late 20s.

I should learn more.
I should read more.
I should code more.
I should watch TV less.

I should go outside more.
I should talk to strangers more.
I should travel more.

But then again, shouldn't we all? How can we possibly get to a point where we should no longer improve anything?

p.s. This tends to be the bulk of my inner monologue, especially at night while lying in bed. This is why I tend to be driven for self improvement.

p.p.s. I also tend to meet these resolutions poorly. I find it easy to make my future self have to deal with. It's always easier to say what other people should do.

p.p.p.s. The worst part is that I know how to accomplish all of these. We all do. So why don't we do it?
